Flagship Research Project
LungGuard- Multimodal AI for Early Lung Cancer Detection

LungGuard is a research-grade multimodal deep learning system designed to support early lung cancer detection using-

  • Chest X-ray imaging
  • Structured clinical and demographic data
  • Unstructured medical text

The system implements early, late, and hybrid fusion strategies, combined with Grad-CAM and SHAP explainability techniques to ensure clinical transparency and trust.

This work reflects my core belief: high-performance AI must remain interpretable when deployed in high-stakes domains.

How I Think About AI

  • Models should justify decisions, not just output predictions.

These principles guide my research, writing, and system design.
